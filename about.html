About EvoTrends

We Commercialize
Complex Technologies

EvoTrends is a strategic partner for deep-tech and R&D-driven companies that need to move beyond innovation and enter real markets.

We specialize in turning technologies into structured products, building the right partnerships, securing funding, and opening access to international markets. Our work starts where standard marketing and consulting stop — in environments where technology is complex, markets are unclear, and traditional go-to-market approaches do not work.

📊

💰
Funding Ecosystems

EU programs, Horizon Europe, investor networks, VC and grant mechanisms

🌍
International Market Access

Real presence and networks across Europe, Asia, Africa and CIS

Our role is to bridge the gap between science and market reality. This includes:

  • ▸ Transforming technologies into clear, communicable value propositions
  • ▸ Structuring products and defining concrete use cases
  • ▸ Building strategic partnerships across industry, public sector, and research
  • ▸ Securing funding through EU programs, investors and VC
  • ▸ Supporting market entry across Europe and internationally

Our Approach

We apply a structured, analytical approach to commercialization. Instead of selling services, we deconstruct the challenge first.

  • ▸ Deconstruct technologies to identify real, defensible market value
  • ▸ Design scalable business and product models
  • ▸ Align technical development with market expectations and funding requirements
  • ▸ Reduce uncertainty and strategic risk at every stage

This approach is especially effective for early-stage deep-tech companies, R&D teams moving toward commercialization, and industrial innovations entering new markets.

Experience

EvoTrends has worked across multiple industries and markets, with a strong focus on complex and non-standard environments. Selected experience includes:

  • ▸ Commercializing deep-tech innovations in environmental and industrial sectors
  • ▸ Acting as strategic commercialization partner for R&D companies (e.g. FIDCHEM)
  • ▸ Co-developing AI-driven products such as Resto-AI
  • ▸ Building international R&D consortia for EU funding programs
  • ▸ Supporting projects across Europe, Asia, Africa and CIS markets
  • ▸ Delivering projects under NDA in industrial, AI and high-tech domains

Who We Work With

  • ▸ Deep-tech startups with proven technology but unclear market path
